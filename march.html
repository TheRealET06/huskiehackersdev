March Schedule

The Huskie Hackers program, by the Office of Innovation at Northern Illinois University, is a unique initiative to support and develop student talent through active participation in the Major League Hacking (MLH) Hackathon season.

Week 1 (March 3 - 7): Introduction to Cloud Computing

Wednesday, March 5 (3 PM - 5 PM):

  • What is Cloud Computing? Introduce the basic concepts, benefits, and deployment models (public, private, hybrid).
  • Service Models Overview: Discuss Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS).

Thursday, March 6 (3 PM - 5 PM):

  • Major Cloud Providers: Provide an overview of leading providers like AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform.
  • Setting Up Free Accounts: Guide members through creating free-tier accounts on a chosen cloud platform.

Friday, March 7 (2 PM - 4 PM):

  • Kick-off for Global Hack Week: Discuss the schedule, objectives, and expectations for the upcoming week.
  • Team Formation: Organize members into teams based on interests and skills.

Week 2 (March 10 - 14): Global Hack Week [OPTIONAL]

Online: Spend a week building awesome cloud-based hacks. Boost your rank!

Week 3 (March 17 - 21): Cloud Networking and Hands-On Labs

Wednesday, March 19 (3 PM - 5 PM):

  • Introduction to Serverless Computing: Learn about serverless architectures and services like AWS Lambda or Azure Functions.

Thursday, March 20 (3 PM - 5 PM):

  • What is Cloud Networking? Provide an overview of how networking operates within cloud environments.
  • Virtual Private Clouds (VPCs): Introduce the concept of VPCs and their purpose in isolating resources.

Friday, March 21 (2 PM - 4 PM):

  • Hands-On Lab: Deploy a simple web application on a cloud platform to reinforce learning.

Week 4 (March 24 - 28): Exploring Cloud Services

Wednesday, March 26 (3 PM - 5 PM):

  • Introduction to Cloud Storage: Discuss different storage options like object storage, file storage, and block storage.

Thursday, March 27 (3 PM - 5 PM):

  • Introduction to Cloud Databases: Explore managed database services and their benefits.

Friday, March 28 (2 PM - 4 PM):

  • Demo Day: Project Demonstrations.
